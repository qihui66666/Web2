河北师范大学（Hebei Normal University）位于河北省会石家庄市，由中华人民共和国教育部与河北省人民政府共建，是一所具有百年历史和光荣传统的省属重点大学，河北省重点支持的国家一流大学建设一层次高校，是中国较早成立、规模较大的高等师范院校之一，入选卓越教师培养计划、中西部高校基础能力建设工程、国家建设高水平大学公派研究生项目、国家“特色重点学科项目”建设高校、新工科研究与实践项目、国家级大学生创新创业训练计划、全国深化创新创业教育改革示范高校、教育部来华留学示范基地建设高校。 河北师大最早起源于1902年创建于北京的顺天府学堂和1906年创建于天津的北洋女师范学堂。1996年6月，原河北师范大学、河北师范学院与创建于1952年的河北教育学院、创建于1984年的河北职业技术师范学院合并，组建成新的河北师范大学。2011年9月，原各校区迁至现址实现集中办学。2014年11月，河北省人民政府、教育部决定共建河北师范大学。 截至2020年4月，学校新校区占地1829亩，馆藏图书303万册，中外文数据库67个；设25个专业学院，1个独立学院（汇华学院），有博士学位授权一级学科11个，博士专业学位授权点1个；硕士学位授权一级学科29个，硕士专业学位授权点14个；博士后科研流动站9个，本科专业95个；有在职教职工2598人，在校本科生24303人，硕士研究生4837人，博士研究生366人，成人教育学生10592人。化学学科、植物学与动物学学科进入ESI世界排名前1%。 Hebei Normal University University is located in Shijiazhuang, the capital of Hebei Province. It is jointly constructed by the Ministry of education of the people's Republic of China and the people's Government of Hebei Province. It is a provincial key university with a history of 100 years and a glorious tradition. It is one of the first-class national universities supported by Hebei Province. It is one of the early established and large-scale normal universities in China. It has been selected into the excellent teacher training program, Chinese and western Ministry of education's basic capacity building project, the national construction of high-level university public postgraduates project, national "characteristic key discipline project" construction universities, new engineering research and practice project, national innovation and entrepreneurship training plan for college students, national innovation and entrepreneurship education reform demonstration universities, and the Ministry of education's overseas study demonstration base construction university. Hebei Normal University originated from shuntianfu school in Beijing in 1902 and Beiyang normal school in Tianjin in 1906. In June 1996, the former Hebei Normal University and Hebei Normal University merged with Hebei Institute of education founded in 1952 and Hebei Vocational and technical normal college founded in 1984 to form a new Hebei Normal University. In September 2011, the original campuses were moved to the current site to realize centralized school running. In November 2014, Hebei Provincial People's government and Ministry of Education decided to jointly build Hebei Normal University. As of April 2020, the new campus of the University covers an area of 1829 mu, has a collection of 3.03 million books, and 67 Chinese and foreign language databases; it has 25 professional colleges and 1 Independent College (Huihua College), including 11 first-class disciplines and 1 doctor's degree authorization point; 29 first-class disciplines authorized for master's degree, 14 authorized master's degree; and 9 mobile post doctoral research stations, which are undergraduate programs There are 2598 in-service teaching staff, 24303 undergraduates, 4837 postgraduates, 366 doctoral students
